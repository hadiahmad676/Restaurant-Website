About Our Restaurant

Salt & Pepper Restaurant and Pub was founded in 2020 by a Ghanaian business man who seeked to create a space where people could enjoy delicious food whilst being in a homey environment. Salt & Pepper offers tasty meal options, from local Ghanaian dishes to Chinese cuisine and more. We have huge TV displays to showcase sporting events, extra cooling airconditioners, a wide range of alcohol, and so much more. For more inquiries, contact 0244643090.
